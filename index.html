About

I'm Nevin Rutabanzibwa — an incoming Finance & Accounting student at Xavier University, building a foundation in financial modeling, accounting principles, and consulting frameworks before day one of my freshman year.

My work is shaped by a conviction that discipline and preparation compound — the same way interest does. Every project here is deliberate practice: real scenarios, real outputs, real standards.
